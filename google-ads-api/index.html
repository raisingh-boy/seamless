Our Internal Tool

We use the Google Ads API to power this internal campaign management and reporting tool, used only by Seamless administrators. This includes:

  • Creating and managing campaigns for our workshops, intensives, retreats, and online courses
  • Monitoring campaign performance (impressions, clicks, cost, conversions)
  • Researching and planning keywords for our own campaigns
  • Reporting on advertising activity to support internal planning and decision-making

Access is limited to internal users — Seamless administrators only. We do not provide API access to third parties, and we do not manage advertising accounts on behalf of other organizations or individuals.

We do not sell, share, or transfer Google Ads data to unrelated third parties.
