Google Ads API

This page explains how the Seamless platform uses the Google Ads API.

Purpose of Use

Seamless uses the Google Ads API to support advertising management and performance analysis for workshops, educational programs, festivals, retreats, and community projects.

Core Functionalities

  • Campaign Management — Supporting the management of advertising campaigns related to events and educational programs.
  • Performance Tracking — Monitoring advertising metrics such as clicks, impressions, and advertising costs.
  • Conversion Analysis — Measuring registrations, event participation, and campaign effectiveness.

Data Safety

Google Ads functionality is used to support advertising management and performance analysis within the Seamless platform. All advertising actions are initiated and controlled by authorized users. We do not sell, share, or transfer Google Ads data to data brokers or unrelated third parties. Users can disconnect their Google Ads account and revoke access permissions at any time.
