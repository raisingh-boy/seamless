What is Floorwork

Moving on the floor —
a practice of its own

Floorwork is the art of moving on the ground — rolling, sliding, shifting weight, transitioning between levels. It's a complete movement practice that anyone can do, anywhere.

Keys to Floorwork is a video course with 24 detailed lessons. Each lesson breaks down one movement pattern: how to enter it, how to link it to the next, and how to make it feel natural.

All you need is space to move, a floor, and comfortable clothes. No partner, no experience, no equipment.
