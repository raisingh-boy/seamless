Two courses — one foundation

Floorwork and Contact Improvisation aren't two separate paths — they're the same practice from different angles. Floorwork teaches your body to move through space on its own: weight, momentum, transitions between levels. Contact Improvisation builds on exactly that — but with another person. Start solo if you have no partner yet. Either way, both courses make you better at the other.

Questions

Do I need any experience?

No. Both courses start from zero. If you can walk, you can start.

Do I need a partner?

Not to start. Floorwork is entirely solo. The CI course introduces partner work, but you can begin alone and find someone as you go.

Which course should I start with?

No partner right now — start with Floorwork. It builds exactly the foundation that makes CI easier. Already have someone to practice with — go straight to CI.

Can I get both?

Yes. They complement each other directly — many students do both.

How long do I have access?

Forever. One purchase, no expiry.
