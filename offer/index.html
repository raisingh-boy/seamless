Who we are

I've been teaching Contact Improvisation for 15 years. Before that, I was a psychologist and educator — so movement and teaching have always been connected for me.

For the last 3 years, we've been building an international community in Bali. People from Europe, Australia, the US, and beyond come here specifically for our jams, workshops, and intensives.

70% of our participants are international. Our month-long Seamless Intensive has produced teachers who now lead their own CI classes and travel to festivals worldwide — in Europe, Australia, across Asia.

This online course builds on the same principles we teach in our live workshops — adapted for self-paced study. It's designed to get you moving on your own or with a partner, and to prepare you for jams and in-person practice.

FAQ
Is this for complete beginners?
Yes. The course starts from zero — no movement or dance experience needed.
How long do I get access?
Course purchase = access forever. Subscription = access while active, cancel anytime.
Is the English translation good?
AI-translated and reviewed. Clear, natural, and accurate.
What if I want to teach CI after this?
After this course you'll be ready to join open jams and continue exploring CI with others. Teaching requires deeper practice over time.
Do you offer certificates?
No. CI is an open-source movement — there are no certificates, and that's by philosophy. What you get is practice, feedback, and a path to keep growing.
