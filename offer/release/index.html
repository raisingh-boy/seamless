What it is

Release tension.
Find ease in your body.

Most of us carry tension we don't notice. In the shoulders, the hips, the jaw — chronic holding patterns that limit how we move and how we feel.

Release work is a set of movement and bodywork practices designed to let go of these patterns. Spherokinesis uses spiral and rotational movements to unlock joints and fascia. Myofascial release practices work directly with connective tissue.

The result: lighter movement, deeper comfort in your body, and a sense of physical freedom that stays with you off the mat.

For dancers, this means better quality of movement. For bodyworkers and massage therapists, it adds a movement dimension to your practice. For anyone — it feels good.
