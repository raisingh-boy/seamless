About Seamless

Seamless is an international movement education and community project based in Bali.

Over the last three years, we have been building a growing international community through Contact Improvisation, movement research, artistic collaboration, workshops, intensives, festivals, retreats, and educational programs.

Participants travel from Europe, Australia, the United States, Asia, and many other countries to join our events. Today, approximately 70% of participants are international visitors.

Our programs bring together dancers, educators, performers, choreographers, and interdisciplinary artists, creating opportunities for learning, artistic exchange, professional development, and community building.

Founded by Roma and Nastya, movement educators and performers with more than 15 years of teaching and performance experience.

Alongside our educational activities, we use internal tools to support event organization, registrations, communication, and promotional activities.
