About Seamless

Seamless is a platform for event organizers, educators, artists, movement practitioners, and community builders.

The platform helps organize workshops, educational programs, festivals, retreats, and community activities. Seamless combines event management, participant registration, community engagement, and marketing tools in a single workspace.

Our mission is to help independent organizers build sustainable communities and promote meaningful events more effectively.

🌍 Community

Our community hosts workshops, intensives, performances, lectures, artist residencies, and educational programs throughout the year. We collaborate with renowned teachers, choreographers, performers, and interdisciplinary artists from around the world, creating opportunities for learning, artistic exchange, and cultural dialogue.

Programs include contemporary dance, contact improvisation, performance art, somatic practices, movement research, and online educational intensives led by internationally recognized artists and educators.

Seamless is built on years of work within the international movement and dance community. The project is founded and led by Roman Singh, a movement educator, performer, and organizer whose work reaches more than 20,000 followers across social media platforms.
