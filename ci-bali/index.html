About

The practice

Seamless started as a Sunday jam in Ubud and grew into a full ecosystem — because Contact Improvisation isn't just a dance form, it's a way of relating: to gravity, to another body, to uncertainty.

It's for dancers who want to go deeper. It's for non-dancers who want to discover what their body can say. It's for therapists, athletes, movers, thinkers — anyone curious about the conversation between bodies.

— Roman, founder of Seamless
