About

🌿 Seamless

Seamless is a movement practice rooted in Contact Improvisation, floorwork, and release technique. It's a playground for curious bodies — where dance meets somatics, improvisation meets anatomy, and you stop trying to look good and start feeling real.

Founded by Roman in Ubud, Bali, Seamless grew from weekly jams into a full ecosystem: beginner courses, intensives, online programs, and private coaching. The practice is for everyone — professional dancers, movers who never danced, therapists, athletes, and anyone who wants to explore what their body can say.

Beyond the physical, Seamless is building Seamless Universe — an interactive knowledge graph that maps the connections between movement, awareness, philosophy, and science. Think Wikipedia meets 3D galaxy of embodied knowledge.

❓ FAQ

Do I need a partner?
No. You can come alone. Partners rotate during jams. In courses you work with different people.
What should I wear?
Comfortable clothes for movement (leggings, shorts, t-shirt). No shoes. Knee pads optional but helpful.
I'm a beginner. Is this for me?
Absolutely. CI for Beginners course is designed for complete newcomers. Jams welcome all levels.
Can I attend if I'm injured?
Yes, with awareness. CI is adaptable. Let your partner and the facilitator know before starting.

Ready to move?

Come to a jam, take a course, or book a session. First time? Start with CI for Beginners.
